About us

Mubiru Wholesale and Retail Shop is a grocery store located in Kansanga Market, owned and operated by Mr. Mubiru Abdullah, a 55-year-old businessman.

The shop specializes in wholesale food items such as beans, groundnuts, and posho flour, while also retailing various household kitchen essentials.

Mr. Mubiru employs two staff members who assist in the daily operations of the business.
